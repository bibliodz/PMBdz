<h1>Financial management</h1>
<p>This module allows you to manage financial transactions related to member's subscriptions:</p>
<ul>
<li>membership
<li>fines on overdue items
<li>loans against payment for one or more categories of documents
</ul>
<p>If you have chosen the simple subscription type, the rates are displayed in <a href='admin.php?categ=empr&sub=categ'>Administration &gt; Borrowers &gt; Categories</a>.</p>
<p>If you have chosen simple loan, the rates are displayed in <a href='admin.php?categ=docs&sub=typdoc'>Administration &gt; Items &gt; Media Types</a>.</p>
