<b>WARNING, this tool adds records if necessary and above all copies.
<br />These will be added and marked as made available by a lending library.</b>
<br /><br />

The file must be in UNIMARC format, zone 995 will take care of your copies.<br /><br />
<ul>
<li>The import is done in three steps:
<OL>
<LI>Upload your file,</LI>
<LI>Preload your file,</LI>
<LI>Transfer into the records database</LI>
</OL></li>
<br /><li>Instead of uploading your file via your web browser, you may send it directly to the server by other means (FTP for example). The file must be named <i>"!!nom_fic!!"</i> and located in admin/import. In this case, you should jump right to the <b>"!!nom_bouton!!"</b>.</li>
</ul>
