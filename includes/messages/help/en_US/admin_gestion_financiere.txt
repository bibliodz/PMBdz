<h1>financial management of the borrowers</h1>
<p>With this module, you'll manage the financial operations related to the borrowers:
</p>
<ul>
<li>subscriptions
<li>fines due to delay in loan return
<li>paying loan for one or several document category
</ul>
<p>If you chose the simple management of subscription, they can be seen in  
<a href='admin.php?categ=empr&sub=categ'>Administration &gt; Borrowers &gt; Categories</a>.</p>
<p>If you chose the simple management of Loans, they can be seen in 
<a href='admin.php?categ=docs&sub=typdoc'>Administration &gt; Items &gt; Materials</a>.</p>
